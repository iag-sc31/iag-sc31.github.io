Paul Melchior's medal

The Paul Melchior's medal awards some outstanding scientists who have had an extraordinary experience and influence in the Earth Tidal Community.

The previous awardees are:
  • Paul Melchior (1997, Bruxelles, Belgium)
  • Hans-Georg Wenzel (2000, Mizusawa, Japan)
  • John Goodkind (2004, Ottawa, Canada)
  • Bernard Ducarme, Tadahiro Sato (2008, Jena, Germany)
  • Houtze Hsu (2013, Warsaw, Poland)
